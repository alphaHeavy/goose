        <div id="article-body" class="article content body clearfix">
          <div class="padded">
                <p>Though <a href="http://fortune.com/2017/04/13/jpmorgan-1q-earnings-ma-trading/">JPMorgan</a> CEO <a href="http://fortune.com/2017/04/04/jpmorgan-jamie-dimon-donald-trump-train-wreck/">Jamie Dimon</a> says he totally disagrees with President Trump&#8217;s decision to withdraw the United States from the <a href="http://fortune.com/2017/06/02/paris-agreement-factchecking-trump/">Paris climate agreement</a>, the bank head is breaking with other corporate leaders by remaining a councilor to the White House.</p>
                <p>&#8220;I absolutely disagree with the Administration&#8221; on its choice to pull out of the landmark accord on climate change, Dimon said in a statement Friday, &#8220;but we have a responsibility to engage our elected officials to work constructively and advocate for policies that improve people&#8217;s lives and protect our environment.&#8221;</p>
                <p>Dimon is one of many CEOs to serve as a member of the White House&#8217;s <a href="http://fortune.com/2017/01/30/trumps-immigrant-ban-puts-his-ceo-allies-in-a-tough-spot/">Strategic and Policy Forum</a>, and unlike SpaceX and Tesla entrepreneur <a href="http://fortune.com/2017/06/01/elon-musk-trump-paris-agreement/">Elon Musk</a> and <a href="http://fortune.com/2017/03/08/disney-iger-shareholders-meeting-trump/">Disney head Bob Iger</a>, he doesn&#8217;t intend to leave that table, a spokesperson for JPMorgan confirmed with <a href="http://money.cnn.com/2017/06/02/investing/jamie-dimon-trump-paris-decision/index.html?iid=hp-stack-dom" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">CNN Money</a>.</p>
                <p>This isn&#8217;t the first dust-up Dimon has endured as a member of the White House&#8217;s business council. Facing critiques for his participation, he told shareholders at <a href="http://fortune.com/2017/05/17/jamie-dimon-jpmorgan-donald-trump/">JPMorgan&#8217;s</a> annual meeting: &#8220;Id try to help any President of the U.S. because Im a patriot.&#8221;</p>
